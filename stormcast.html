The Stormcast Eternal Army

The Stormcast Eternals, once great mortal human heroes, have undergone the Reforging to become divine living weapons of the God-King Sigmar. A Stormcast can stand upwards of 9 feet tall, though the average is 7 feet 6 inches, with a physique sculpted by the hands of divine smiths.
